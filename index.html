Privacy Policy

Drinking Pal (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Drinking Pal does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data. The App does not require an account, and we do not track your activity, behavior, or location.

2. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, advertising networks, or any form of cross-app or third-party tracking. All usage happens locally on your device.

3. Data Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised. The App runs entirely offline, and no data is transmitted to any server.

4. Children’s Privacy

Drinking Pal is intended for individuals of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under this age. If you are not of legal drinking age, please do not use the App.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices or for legal or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted within the App or on our website.
